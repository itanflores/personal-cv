About Me

Hello, I’m Manuel Itandeui Flores Ortega, an experienced Project Manager and IT professional with a robust educational background and diverse expertise in operations, quality management, and IT systems. I hold Master’s degrees in Operations and Quality Management as well as Systems of Quality and Productivity from UNIR Universidad de La Rioja, in addition to a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems from UVM Universidad del Valle de México. My academic achievements are complemented by a variety of professional certifications, including in Microsoft Azure, Oracle Cloud Architect, and ITIL, among others.

Professionally, I’ve spent over a decade at Redes KIO, where I currently serve as a Project Manager, Operations Coordinator, and Technical Leader. My role involves overseeing large-scale IT projects, improving service delivery through innovative technological solutions, and leading a team of IT specialists to ensure high standards of service and security compliance are met consistently.

These roles equipped me with a profound understanding of IT systems and operations within high-stakes environments, enabling me to effectively manage and optimize complex systems.

I am passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and am always looking for new challenges that can utilize my skills in technology management and project leadership. My professional journey is marked by a commitment to continuous learning and improvement, striving to stay at the forefront of technological advancements and best practices.
